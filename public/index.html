Hi! I'm a digital product design lead specializing in launching early-stage user applications and product innovation.

I'm currently on sabbatical studying personal computing and artificial intelligence.

Values

Good design is achieved organically. While there is value in top-down approaches, I believe skilled craftspeople perform best when they're personally invested. With this in mind, I strive to design products that value inclusively and sustainability in order to build delightful experiences that endure time.

Work

Recently, I've worked with startups Arthur and Datavore to deliver new products. Previously, I managed user experience initiatives at Splashlight's innovation lab for large retail clients such as Eileen Fisher, Aldo, and Under Armour. I've also worked across art and technology at Olu and Company and digital teams at New Museum and Gagosian Gallery.
